Research is a tree of bets, and most bets fail — but today every /trainlint:plan run overwrites the one plan a project has, and viz only ever renders that single living plan. So the moment you try a second direction, or a bet doesn't pan out, the previous thinking is gone: there's no way to see, compare, or learn from the attempts a repo has accumulated. Throwing away the failed bets throws away the most useful record you have. This project makes each plan a durable, append-only proposal you can manage instead of overwrite.
