Turn trainlint's single mutable per-project plan into a managed, append-only collection of PROPOSALS — each proposal a frozen snapshot of a whole plan (one research bet), carrying its own plan-level lifecycle status, never rolled back (a failed bet stays on the record with its outcome), all coexisting FLAT with no "active" one privileged — and make the viz report the surface that lists, filters, diffs, and opens them. The four pillars: the frozen snapshot (what a proposal IS), the flat no-active collection (how many coexist), the plan-level lifecycle status (what state each carries), and the viz management surface (how you steer them). DONE = one repo holds N proposals on disk; viz renders them as a manageable index + the existing per-proposal report + a pairwise decision-level diff; failed bets are still readable; and the online hooks have a defined target without any single active project.
