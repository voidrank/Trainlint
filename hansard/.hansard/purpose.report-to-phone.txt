The harness's end product is report-shaped context that replaces chatbot transcripts: humans read it as a report, parallel agent sessions (a swarm) warm-start from it, and the substrate underneath (decisions, quiz answers, feedback, anchors) doubles as collected data for eval benchmarks + training while the harness self-evolves from the feedback. The phone delivery is the operator's always-available read-and-annotate surface of that context — every close puts the full interactive report in their hand, and their highlights/answers flow back into the data flywheel.
